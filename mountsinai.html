005 : Mount Sinai Health Hackathon


Oct 2019
Ichan School of Medicine at Mount Sinai
Manhattan, NY

In 2019, I competed at the Mount Sinai Health Hackathon. Our goal was to design a product that would would improve an apsect of their patients lives with a focus on artificial intelligence. My team developped an application and analytics library for patients suffering with Type 2 diabetes. Our primary focus was on patients that did not qualify for an automated tracking system. I focued on configuring the database and connecting the analytics to the UI as well as ensuring any sensitive information was encrypted in transit. After the base of our app was done, I worked on building an Alexa command set for patients to report their data and hear analytic results.

Primary Tools Used:

  • Python
  • ReactJS
  • Node.js
