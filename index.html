About Jared

Jared Gridley, age 20, is an undergraduate Computer Science and Mathematics student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Jared has worked as an undergraduate researcher with Dr. Nidhi Rastogi at the Rensselaer IDEA, focusing on relationship extraction and trustworthiness estimation. Currently, Jared works as a Data Science Intern at the Federal Bureau of Investigation where he has worked on developping models for sentiment analysis, image recognition and classification, and data mining. In his free time, Jared likes to run and work on various side projects, like restoring vintage cars and CTF challanges.
